Task Description

You will be shown a series of photos in sets of 3 at a time.

1 image will be on top, and 2 below.
Click one of the 2 bottom images that looks more similar to the top one, and you'll see the next image triplet.

After 500 pairings (it takes about 2-5 min), you'll be given a completion code. Your scores will be compared to majority vote for accuracy. Please submit the completion code.
